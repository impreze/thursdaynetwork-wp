Membership Information

Thank you for your interest in joining the Thursday Network! Thursday Network (TN) is an organization committed to uplifting the Metropolitan DC area while fostering the personal and professional development of our members.

Our membership is comprised of young professionals ages 21-40. We look forward to your participation in continuing our efforts to make a difference in the Greater Washington DC community. Our members play an active role in our community-service projects and are encouraged to participate in at least three community service events per year.

In order to become a member, you must complete an application and pay $100 dues, which entitles you to membership of the National Urban League, Greater Washington Urban League, Thursday Network and National Urban League Young Professionals for one year.

All potential members are encouraged to attend a General Body Meeting and New Member Open House before joining Thursday Network. Please check our Thursday Network Google Calendar for our upcoming General Body Meetings and New Member Open Houses. For more information contact membership@thursdaynetwork.org.

Membership Benefits

  • Discounted registration to National Urban League & NULYP annual conferences
  • Discounted or free admission to Thursday Network and partner organization events
  • Membership into the Greater Washington Urban League, National Urban League, and NULYP
  • Certified Greater Washington Urban League Speakers Bureau Member
  • Discounted ZipCar Membership and rental rates
  • Professional Development and Community Service Hours Certificates
  • Free tickets to advance movie screenings, sporting events, and other local events
  • Exclusive “Members Only” Newsletter and listserv with other members-only offers and information
  • Volunteer and network with like-minded individuals who are creating positive change in the community
  • Access to dynamic speakers via professional workshops and seminars
  • Opportunities to cultivate a mentor relationship with local leaders and established professionals in the Greater Washington Metropolitan area
  • Exclusive invitations to local social and professional networking events
